Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Cruise SEO

1. Who We Are

Cruise SEO ("we", "us", or "our") is an AI-powered SEO platform. Our service is accessible at cruiseseo.site. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and information generated through your use of the service:

  • Account information — your name and email address, collected through Clerk authentication
  • Content you create — keywords, blog posts, and SEO data you enter or generate within the app (stored locally in your browser)
  • Website data — your WordPress site URL and connection credentials (stored locally in your browser)
  • Payment information — billing details processed securely by Stripe; we never see or store your card numbers
  • Usage data — how you interact with the app, which features you use, and error logs

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Cruise SEO service
  • To process your subscription and manage billing
  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • To generate AI content based on your inputs via the Anthropic API
  • To send transactional emails (account confirmation, billing receipts)
  • To respond to support requests and improve user experience

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Cruise SEO:

  • Clerk (clerk.com/privacy) — user authentication and account management
  • Anthropic (anthropic.com/privacy) — AI content generation via the Claude API; your prompts and generated text are processed by Anthropic subject to their privacy policy
  • Stripe (stripe.com/privacy) — payment processing for Pro subscriptions
  • Vercel (vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy) — hosting and serverless functions

We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

5. Data Storage

Most of your working data (keywords, posts, settings, brand info) is stored locally in your browser using localStorage and does not leave your device unless you explicitly use features that connect to external services (e.g., publishing to WordPress).

Account data is stored securely by Clerk. Payment data is stored securely by Stripe. We store minimal server-side data: only your subscription status linked to your Clerk user ID.

6. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies only as required for authentication (Clerk session cookies) and to remember your preferences. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. If you cancel your account, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or financial compliance reasons.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to processing of your data in certain circumstances

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, authentication via Clerk, and server-side API key management. No method of internet transmission is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

10. Children's Privacy

Cruise SEO is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, by email. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

Questions or requests about this policy? Email us at hello@cruiseseo.site. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
