FAQ

Common questions

Does it really publish automatically to my WordPress site?
Yes — once you connect your WordPress site during onboarding, Cruise SEO publishes posts directly. No copy-paste, no logging in to WordPress. Each post is queued for your review before going live if you prefer.
Will the content sound like it was written by a robot?
No — during onboarding you share your brand voice, tone, and expertise. Our AI is trained specifically on your style, so posts read like they came from you. Many customers' clients can't tell the difference.
How long until I see results on Google?
Most customers see measurable keyword movement within 6–10 weeks. Significant traffic increases typically appear at the 90-day mark. SEO compounds over time — the longer you run it, the better the results.
Do I need any technical knowledge?
None at all. If you can answer questions about your business and connect a WordPress site (we walk you through it step by step), you're done. We handle all the technical SEO, writing, and publishing.
Can I cancel any time?
Yes, always. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel from your dashboard in one click and you won't be charged again. Your published content stays live on your site regardless.
What if I want to edit a post before it goes live?
Every post appears in your dashboard before publishing. You can read, edit, approve or reject it. Most customers approve posts as-is, but the option is always there.
