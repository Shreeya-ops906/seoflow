FAQ

Got questions?

Will the content sound like my brand? +
Yes. During onboarding SEOFlow builds a brand profile from your business details and tone preferences. Every post matches your voice and audience — not a generic AI template.
Which platforms do you support? +
SEOFlow connects to WordPress via the REST API — which powers over 40% of all websites. Wix, Squarespace, and Webflow support is on the roadmap.
Can I review posts before they go live? +
Yes. Every post enters a review queue by default. Approve, edit, or reject with one click. Once you trust the quality, switch to fully automatic — most users do within the first fortnight.
How many posts per week does it publish? +
You set the schedule. Most users publish 3 times a week. Growth plan is up to 30 posts/month; Agency is unlimited.
Will Google penalise my site for AI content? +
No. Google evaluates content quality and helpfulness — not how it was produced. SEOFlow generates original, well-structured, genuinely useful articles. That's what Google ranks.
Is there a free trial? +
The Starter plan is free forever — no credit card needed. Growth and Agency plans include a 14-day free trial.
