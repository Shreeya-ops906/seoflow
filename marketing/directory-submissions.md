### LONG DESCRIPTION (~500 words — use for G2, Capterra, GetApp)
```
CruiseSEO is an all-in-one AI SEO platform that helps small businesses, freelancers, and agencies build Google rankings without needing an in-house SEO team or expensive agency retainer.

THE PROBLEM
Most small businesses know SEO is important — but they don't have the time to write blog posts every week, the budget to hire an agency, or the technical knowledge to optimise their site. They either do nothing or publish inconsistent, low-quality content that never ranks.

THE SOLUTION
CruiseSEO automates the entire SEO content workflow from keyword to published post.

Enter a keyword → AI generates a full, structured SEO blog post → publish directly to WordPress in one click. The whole process takes under 2 minutes.

CORE FEATURES

AI Blog Generator
Generate publish-ready SEO posts in seconds. Each post includes an SEO-optimised title, meta description, heading structure (H1–H4), introduction, body sections with internal links, and a call-to-action. The AI uses your brand voice, industry, and keywords to ensure every post sounds like you.

WordPress Direct Publishing
Connect your WordPress site with your username and application password. Posts are published directly — no copy-pasting, no formatting issues.

Rank Tracker
Add target keywords and track your estimated Google position over time. Get AI-powered opportunity scoring so you know which keywords to prioritise.

Site SEO Audit
Run a full technical SEO audit on any URL. Get a health score out of 100 with specific recommendations to fix issues affecting your rankings.

Social Media Integration
Generate LinkedIn, Twitter, and Facebook snippets from any post. Connect LinkedIn via OAuth and post with one click. Auto-post when you publish is optional.

Content Grader
Paste any URL or article content and get an SEO score with specific improvement recommendations.

Topic Clusters & Competitor Analysis
Identify content gaps in your niche and discover what your competitors are ranking for.

Backlink Monitor
Track your inbound links, monitor dofollow vs nofollow ratio, and get AI-generated link building opportunity ideas.

Bulk Generation (Scale plan)
Generate up to 10 SEO posts at once from a list of keywords — ideal for agencies or sites that need to build topical authority quickly.

PLANS & PRICING
• 3-day free trial — full access, no card charge until day 4
• Pro — $49/month — 15 posts/month, 3 WordPress sites, all core features
• Scale — $149/month — 60 posts/month, 10 WordPress sites, bulk generation, AI image generation

WHO IT'S FOR
• Small business owners who need a consistent SEO presence
• Freelance marketers managing multiple client sites
• WordPress agencies looking to add SEO content services
• E-commerce stores that need regular blog content
• Local service businesses (lawyers, dentists, tradespeople) who want to rank locally
```
